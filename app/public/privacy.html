Introduction

InHand is a salary estimation and decision-support web app for Indian employees. It helps you estimate in-hand salary and understand your salary structure through high-level calculations and planning views.

Not financial advice: InHand is not an investment advisory service, loan broking service, tax filing service, or payroll processor. It does not sell financial products.

Data We Collect

Depending on how you use InHand, we may collect and process:

  • Account data: email address and authentication data (handled via Supabase Auth).
  • Profile data: display name, company, role (optional).
  • Salary inputs: salary structure/CTC components, assumptions you enter, and offer comparison inputs.
  • Uploaded documents (optional): salary/offer PDFs you select. These are processed in-browser for extraction/review.
  • Usage/session data: first-party cookies to restore your last active workspace and basic UI state.
  • Billing identifiers (Premium): Razorpay subscription/payment identifiers and status required to enable Premium.
Consent for upload/processing (DPDPA): If you choose to upload/select a document or enter salary data, you consent to InHand processing that data solely for salary estimation, comparisons, and related decision-support features. You can withdraw consent by deleting your account or by requesting erasure (see “Your Rights”).

How We Use Your Data

We use your data only for the purposes below:

  • Provide the service: run salary estimations, show breakdowns, offer comparisons, and planners.
  • Account management: sign-in, profile, and Premium entitlement.
  • Security: protect accounts, prevent abuse, and maintain service reliability.
  • Session continuity: restore your last active workspace using first-party cookies like inhand_last_salary_session.

No selling: We do not sell your personal data.

Razorpay Payments

We use Razorpay for secure payments and subscriptions. When you pay:

  • Razorpay collects card/UPI details directly. We do not store your card/UPI credentials.
  • We receive only payment confirmation details such as amount, status, and Razorpay payment/subscription identifiers.
  • You consent to Razorpay processing your payment data for completing the transaction.
  • Razorpay’s privacy policy applies: razorpay.com/privacy

Your Rights (DPDPA)

As a Data Principal, you may request:

  • Access: a copy/summary of personal data we process about you.
  • Correction: update inaccurate profile information.
  • Erasure: request deletion/erasure of your personal data, subject to lawful retention for billing and dispute handling.
  • Withdraw consent: stop processing by deleting your account and/or requesting erasure.

Erasure process: Email us with your account email and request. We may verify identity before acting.

Data Security

We use reasonable security practices including HTTPS encryption in transit, access controls, and least-privilege principles. Payment data security for cards/UPI is handled by Razorpay.
